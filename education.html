Kindergarten

I started going to Kindergarten at the age of 4 years old until 6 years old.
I remembered when my mother told me I was going to Kindergarten, I was very excited.
I was excited to learn about numbers, shapes, math and words.
My mother enrolled me in a Chinese Kindergarten School in Penang because she wants me
to learn other languages besides English and Malay. Also, it was easier for my parents,
since they worked near my Kindergarten.
I remembered my first Mandarin teacher, she taught me everything and I was always successful
in her class. I learned on how to count to ten in Mandarin and later will be awarded with stickers and candy.


Primary School

(2007-2011)

My old primary school was Sekolah Kebangsaan Seri Permai, Penang. I went
there for 3 months only before my family decided to move to Bukit Mertajam.
My new primary school is Sekolah Kebangsaan Jalan Baharu, Bukit Mertajam and it
was located near my grandmother house, so everytime after school
I would go back to my grandmother's house instead of living with my parents because of work.
Being an introvert, I still remember how it was hard for me to find new friends since everyone
has already had their own clique. But one day, a girl named Husna approach me and we instantly becoming best friend.
Being friends with Husna made me becoming a new person. I was no longer alone and shy,
I became more active with academic and curricular activities. Me and Husna decided to joined
the Netball team and we will always practicing after school. Unfortunately, Husna moved to
Kulim on Standard 5, so I was left alone again until I finish my UPSR examination. Luckily,
I was able to get 4A's for my UPSR in Bahasa Melayu, English, Science and Mathematics.


High School

(2012-2016)

After receiving my UPSR results, I decided to not going to any boarding school,
instead going to a normal high school near my house. I went to Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan
Permai Indah. I took a bus every morning to school. I met a lot of good friends, too.
Most people said that high school is the best years of your life and I agreed with it.
I learned so much about myself and so much about life. I learned what the words family, love,
betrayal, law and life meant. My personality hasn't changed, I'm still the introvert girl.
But I'm always thankful to have a group of friends who always being tolerant and always comforting
me.

These are my high school's bestfriends. Some of them have already working somewhere
and some were still studying like me. When i think about my high school memories,
there are many things that come to mind. Most of the good memories involved my good friends and most of the bad memories involved just me.
Some examples of good memories in high school is just like going to the different sports games with my friends.
My friends and I attended many of the school football games, rugby games and some netball games.
Since junior year, I was a shy, unmotivated young girl that didn't really know what she was getting into.
I am leaving high school like a girl who is mature, outgoing, and very sure of herself.
If high school has taught me anything it is that you should always have confidence in yourself.
If you believe in yourself and you believe that you can achieve anything you want in life, good things will happen to you.
Knowing that I am graduating high school and never coming back hasn't really hit me yet.
Those 5 years has been an overall life changing experience that I will never forget.


University

Diploma (2017-2020)

My parents had always told me that it is extremely important to receive a diploma
not only because of acquisition of new knowledge but also because of the different experiences.
After finishing my SPM, I applied for UiTM Diploma in Office Management and got accepted at
UiTM Merbok, Kedah. My experience so far at UiTM Kedah has been a roller coaster ride.
It has had its ups and downs similar to any other new experience to a person. My Diploma experiences
thus far have been amazing, despite the hard things and a very hectic life. Also, it has been
a few months since I last finished my Diploma and waiting to graduate with a cgpa 3.70.

I have learned and experienced so many new things in such a short period of time, and it has gone by so fast.
I am still discovering all the opportunities that university offers me.
UiTM Kedah is a very welcoming place, and from the first day, I felt at home there.
I have made many new friends from many different backgrounds. There are from Kedah, Perlis and
many more and that has allowed me an insight into their cultures.


Degree (2020)

After finishing my Diploma, I applied for Penerapan Sarjana Muda in Office System Management
and I got accepted to continued my Degree at UiTM Machang, Kelantan. As a freshman it takes some time
for me adjusting to get used to the style of learning especially since this pandemic, we have to use the
platform of online learning. But luckily, two of my friends during my Diploma year was there with me,
and coincidentally they're were my classmates so it lessen some of the burden I was facing.
It has been a few months since I started my bachelor's degree, and in just another few months, we're
going to end our second semester. It is definitely has been an experience I will need in the future
and it is better to learn it now than later on down the road.

These are my classmate during my Diploma year who actually were once again my classmate during
my Degree year. However, due to the pandemic season all the university were closed and students were asked
to have an online classes instead. My online learning experience as a student is not so good.
I have to wake up early every day and I'm not a morning person. In addition, online classes is confusing when
the lecturer gives us work mostly since it's easier for me to do work with instructions that the lecturer gives.
Being in a physical classroom makes me only focus on my work, but online learning makes it harder to focus
because I have to also focus on household stuff, and it throws me off.
I would not want to do online learning in the future because it is hard for me to concentrate on my work only.
Nevertheless, UiTM is a place that truly helps shaping one's personality.
It is like another Godsend gift for me. It is one of the best universities in my entire vicinity.
We are truly cared, rightly educated and well socialized here. I myself feel proud of being a student here.
I do take care of what is being taught and what is to learn. I respect my lecturers, my fellow beings,
my university and everything that is associated with it.
